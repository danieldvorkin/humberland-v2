Humberland is for you

If you are looking for a nurturing, loving environment where your child will truly blossom, you’ve found the right place!


Why choose a regular daycare for your child? Why not put them in a Montessori daycare that provides your child with high quality education at an early age. Many experts agree that children are motivated and will learn more between the ages of 0 and 6 years old, than any other time in their lives. Humberland Montessori Academy (HMA) emphasizes independence in order to help provide your child with the foundation for a life-long love of learning and give them the skills they need for greater success in other areas of their life. We are committed to providing an exceptional Montessori education where your child can be creative, explore, and discover. Our positive teaching environment truly feels like an extended family. HMA is locally owned and operated school that’s fully accredited and staffed with loving, caring, experienced instructors.

Focused Education

Our Academy will give your child the basis while instilling the potential for creativity and innovation at an early age. Our goal at HMA is to create a pleasant and nurturing environment where the child is able to reach his/her full potential. We believe education is the foundation for life. The education a child receives at an early age is one of the most important factors that will determine his or her future.


Our professional and experienced teaching staff will guide your child to become the best they can be. Our intimate atmosphere encourages each child to cooperate with one another and build a foundation of trust amongst each other. We also offer extra-curriculum programs that will allow your child to develop new interests, interact with their peers, and help them forge the kind of friendships that will last a lifetime. When you want an alternative approach to the public education system, check out what Humberland Montessori has to offer. We invite you to tour our website to learn more about our special programs. Whether you are looking for a nurturing, caring environment for your toddler, a stimulating and creative program for your pre-schooler, or an enriching and exceptionally high quality education for your school aged child, we encourage you to come visit us. We look forward to welcoming your child at Humberland Montessori Academy.
