About Us

Did you know that Humberland Montessori Academy is operated by a Mom andher two daughters? LuzMarina and her daughters Natalia and Gisela have teamed up and opened up a preschool for children ages 18 months-6 years old. LuzMarina and Natalia are both Montessori Teachers MACTE approved.

Natalia is the supervisor of the school and ensures that day to day operations run smoothly. LuzMarina is the Administrator and overseas everything that goes on in the school. Gisela is an Ontario Certified Teacher with a Masters Degree in education in the Primary Junior Division, and is also the head teacher for the JK-SK class.

Together, they work as a team to make Humberland Montessori Academy a place called home away from home. They have always dreamed of opening up their own school, so they took a leap of faith and purchased HMA. The mom and two daughters are a close nit family who have always been there for each other and have strong family core values providing the most nurturing, loving environment for all children.

Aside from providing a loving environment, their goal is for all children to achieve high academic standards and work above curriculum level. Above all, they believe the most important part is for children to develop from the inside out, build self- confidence, and self-achievement through trial and error. This family truly has a passion for teaching and a love of learning. All parents are encouraged to visit the school, and meet this amazing family.
